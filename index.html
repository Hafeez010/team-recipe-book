Alex's Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Salted Butter (Softened)
  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 3 Cups all-purpose Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Sea Salt
  • 2 Cups Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 375 Farenheit - 190 Celcius.
  2. Mix Flour, Baking Soda and Salt.
  3. Cream together the butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Beat in the Eggs and Vanilla until light (roughly 1 minute)
  5. Mix in the dry inredients until combined
  6. Roll 2-3 tablespoons of dough (at a time) into balls and place them on your cookie sheets (evenly spaced)
  7. Bake for approximately 8-10 minutes. Take them out of the oven as they begin to turn brown
  8. Let them sit on the baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to the cooling rack

Zee's Purin

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 350ml milk
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. For the caramel, heat 2 tablespoons of sugar and cook on a low heat. Divide in ramekins
  2. Heat your milk on low heat until it's just about to boil
  3. Add sugar/sweetener to the milk alongside vanilla
  4. In a separate bowl crack 2eggs and 1 egg yolk and mix
  5. Once the milk has cooled down, slowly pour the mixture into the ramekins
  6. Place covered ramakins (with foil) in a tray and fill the tray with 500ml hot water. Bake at 160°c (ensure sure it’s preheated) for 50minutes. Let it cool and put it in the fridge! :D enjoy <3

Noel's Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Toppings

  • Maple Syrup
  • Fresh Berries
  • Whipped Cream

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat
  3. Pour batter into the pan to form pancakes
  4. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown

Reece's Strawberry Cake

Ingredients

  • 295g cake flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 170g butter
  • 350g sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 120ml milk
  • 300g fresh strawberries

Instructions

  • Puree the strawberries in a blender and leave on medium-low heat in a saucepan
  • Preheat the oven to 180 Celcius
  • Whisk the cake flour and baking powder together and set aside
  • Mix the butter and sugar together for 3 minutes
  • Add eggs and mix for 2 minutes
  • Add vanilla extract and mix for 1 minute
  • Add the cake mix
  • Mix and continuously add milk
  • Add the strawberry puree and mix
  • Bake for 25 minutes
  • Add frosting/decorations as desired
  • Cover cake and store in fridge
